Hello everyone!
We are AdoptNet and we are welcoming all of you to our website.
As we build AdoptNet our original goal
was to find a way to connect between associations for animals
and people who want to adopt a new friend for life.
We work with a number of non-profit organizations in the country
and make it easy for those who want to adopt.
as we connect with more and more association and make a collaboration with them
the search for a pet gets easier and more accessible
We wish you a pleasant search and that you will find a new life member.
